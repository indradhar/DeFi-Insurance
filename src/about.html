About Our Team




We are all very different. We were born in different cities, at different times, we love different music, food, movies. But we have something that unites us all. It is our company. We are its heart. We are not just a team, we are a family.

Getting to know people is hard, but getting to know people online, is even harder.Human beings are not one-dimensional. We all sound, look, behave, and even smell differently, but these traits (some, thankfully) can't be experienced from behind a cold, digital screen.

No matter how advanced technology gets, there will always be a divide keeping us from truly knowing a person we've only spent time with online. Unfortunately for marketers, the same difficulties could be said about brands. In fact, it's often harder to establish a personal connection with brands across the wide expanse of cyberspace.
